Our Mission

We work to create a safe, empowering space for girls and non-binary students in India to explore mathematics confidently. We recognize how gender bias and systemic barriers affect access to STEM for many students, especially non-binary youth. Our goal is to support young learners early‚Äîhelping them discover joy in problem solving, grow into competitive thinkers, explore STEM pathways, and gain access to opportunities including olympiads, mentorship, and international programs.

FAQ

I'm interested but have no math competition experience. Can I still attend?

Of course, you should really attend this competition. In the past, many people have stated that INTEGIRLS gave them an entry into math competitions and they had no idea how fun it was until they attended the event. If you would like any pointers on how to prepare, you can feel free to email us at bangalore@integirls.org. We hope to see you at our event(s)!

I do not have an INTEGIRLS Chapter near my area, but I am interested in participating. Can I still attend?

We encourage students not only from Bangalore/India to attend, but also other areas as well. You may reach out to us at the links provided at the bottom of this page.

Who can I contact if I have any questions?

You can contact us through Email or Instagram at the links provided at the bottom of this page. It may take up to two weeks to get a reply from us through email. If it is urgent, you can message us on Instagram or add "URGENT" to the subject line of your email.

I do not identify as female or non-binary, but I would like to participate. How can I do that?

If you do not identify as female or non-binary, we encourage you to check out other opportunities we may share. You can contact bangalore@integirls.org to find more activities apt for you.

How can I contribute?

You may choose to volunteer for us by emailing us at bangalore@integirls.org or can donate to our non-profit here (link coming soon!). You can also email us at bangalore@integirls.org to receive more information about how you can help. YOUR HELP MAKES A DIFFERENCE TO US.

How many competitions will you conduct throughout the year?

We are targeting to host two competitions a year: one in the fall and one in the spring. More details about these competitions will be available on this website soon.
